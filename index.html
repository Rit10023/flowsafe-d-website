Safeflow-D

Composition: Tamsulosin Hydrochloride 0.4 mg (Modified Release) + Dutasteride 0.5 mg, Film-coated Tablets

Tamsulosin

  • Alpha-blocker: Relaxes muscles in the prostate and bladder neck to ease urination.
  • Symptomatic relief: Improves urine flow, reduces frequency, and helps with nocturia.
  • Dosage: Typically taken once daily, 30 minutes after breakfast.

Dutasteride

  • 5-alpha-reductase inhibitor: Blocks production of DHT, a hormone causing prostate enlargement.
  • Shrinks prostate: Helps improve urine flow and reduce symptoms.
  • Dosage: Typically taken once daily.

Combination Therapy

  • Tamsulosin + Dutasteride offers both immediate and long-term relief from BPH symptoms.

Benefits

  • Improved urine flow and reduced urinary symptoms.
  • Reduced prostate size via hormonal effect.
  • Better quality of life by managing discomfort and improving sleep.

Side Effects

  • Tamsulosin: Dizziness, headache, orthostatic hypotension.
  • Dutasteride: Decreased libido, erectile dysfunction, breast tenderness.

Consultation

  • Always consult a healthcare provider before starting treatment.
  • Inform your doctor about any existing conditions or medications.
  • Report side effects promptly to your doctor.
